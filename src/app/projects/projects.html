Projects

Void Nite (Gaming App)

Void Nite is a gaming application that allows users to play games both online and offline. The platform enables gamers to interact with other players from around the world, creating a connected gaming community. The project focuses on usability, performance, and an engaging gaming experience.

WebWarrior Wizard (Gaming E-commerce Platform)

WebWarrior Wizard is an e-commerce website designed for gamers. It allows users to purchase gaming equipment such as consoles, PlayStations, and accessories needed to play various games. The project focuses on product presentation, user navigation, and a smooth shopping experience.

Amaka Medical (Healthcare Platform)

Amaka Medical is a medical web platform designed to help hospitals and patients connect online. Users can seek medical assistance, consult doctors remotely, and access healthcare services without physically visiting the hospital. Patient records are stored digitally to improve accessibility and efficiency.

Tutorial Haven

Tutorial Haven is an AI-powered website to help students prepare for O level's exams like WAEC,NECO,JAMB etc. students can chat with AI to ask for questions, practice past questions online and also chat with other students and also practice real-life CBT exams.
